# Spotless Blinds Co. - Step-by-step execution guide

## Business name: Spotless Blinds Co.

"Spotless Blinds" directly communicates the outcome. "Blind Spot" has negative connotations. Adding "Co." gives it a premium, established feel.

## Phase 1: Legal and financial setup (Weeks 1-2)

1. Register LLC at bizfileOnline.sos.ca.gov ($70)
2. Get EIN at irs.gov (free, 10 min)
3. Open business bank account
4. Get insurance: General liability ($1M min), Commercial auto, Equipment
5. Get business license from city/county ($50-$150)

## Phase 2: Equipment and training (Weeks 3-6)

**Equipment to purchase:**
- 8-ft ultrasonic blind cleaner (dual tank): $4,000-$7,000 from DirtyBlinds.com or SharperTek
- Used cargo van (Ford Transit Connect or similar): $15,000-$25,000
- Portable generator 3500W: $800-$1,200
- 50-100 gal water tank: $100-$200
- Drying racks and fans: $200-$400
- Ladders, tools, restringing kit: $300-$500
- Cleaning solution (biodegradable, bulk): $150-$300
- Uniforms, drop cloths: $200-$400

**Training:**
- DirtyBlinds.com: free 3-day hands-on training with purchase
- Practice on 10-15 homes (friends/family) before taking paid work

## Phase 3: Digital presence (Weeks 4-6)

1. Website is live at spotless-blinds.vercel.app
2. Set up Google Business Profile (most important marketing asset)
3. Instagram: @spotlessblinds (before/after content)
4. Facebook business page
5. Set up Jobber or Housecall Pro for booking ($30-$49/mo)

## Phase 4: Launch (Weeks 7-12)

1. 15% off for first 20 customers
2. Ask every customer for Google review
3. Build referral partnerships with house cleaners, realtors, designers
4. Google Ads: $500/month targeting "blind cleaning [city]"
5. Nextdoor.com posts in target zip codes

## Phase 5: Scale (Months 6-12)

1. Hire first technician at $25/hr
2. Add window cleaning service
3. Pursue commercial accounts (restaurants, property mgmt)
4. Purchase second ultrasonic unit

## Key numbers

- Average home: 20-35 blinds
- Cleaning time: ~2 min per blind
- Full home job: 2-4 hours on-site
- Average residential ticket: $500-$800
- Luxury home ticket: $1,000-$2,500
- Break-even: 3 jobs per month
- Year 1 revenue at 3 jobs/week: $101,400
- Monthly overhead: ~$2,000
- Monthly take-home at 3 jobs/week: ~$6,400

## Competitors

- Bay Area Blind Cleaners (Millbrae, est. 1972) - pickup/delivery model
- SF Blind Cleaners - San Francisco focused
- COIT Cleaning - franchise, broader service

None target luxury Marin/Walnut Creek with health-first messaging and on-site service.
